Easy Quinoa Salad

Home

Description

This quinoa salad recipe is as delicious as it is nutritious, and easy to prep in just 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa and water
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Limes
  • Seasonings (ground cumin, salt, red pepper flakes, fresh cilantro, and black pepper)
  • Cherry tomatoes and green onions
  • Black beans

Steps

  1. Cook the quinoa.
  2. Make the dressing.
  3. Combine the quinoa with the tomatoes, black beans, and green onions.
  4. Toss the salad with the dressing, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
